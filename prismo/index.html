Key finding

77.8% of AI brand distortion is framing, not fact. AI doesn't get your founding year wrong — it subtly hedges your strengths, inflates your weaknesses, or describes you like your competitor. Fact-checkers can't catch this. Brand.json can. Every correction in our dataset includes the specific framing bias detected and the correction needed.
